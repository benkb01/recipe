Cheesy Lasagna

Home

Description

This lasagna is so tasty, it'll leave your guests hungry for seconds!

All you need is lasagna noodles, some cheeses, sauce, meat, and your favorite toppoings, and you should be good to go.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1/2 Cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • Your favorite herbs and spices
  • 1 large egg
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • Mozzarella or parmeson cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Make meat sauce
  3. Add tomato sauce
  4. While sauce is cooking, make the cheese mixture.
  5. Cook the pasta
  6. Assemble the Lasagna
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes. Remove foil and bake for fifteen more minutes
  8. Let the lasagna cool for at least fifteen minutes
  9. Serve and enjoy!
