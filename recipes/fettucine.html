Fettucine Alfredo

Home

Description

This alfredo is extra creamy, and the herbs leave a bit of an extra kick. It's a perfect meal to serve at Thanksgiving, and a fun way to mix up the boring Spaghetti with red sauce.

Ingredients

  • Fettucine noodles
  • Alfredo sauce
  • Basil and garlic
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. First, heat a large pot of water to boil. Cook the noodles for 7 minutes or until desired tenderness.
  2. Add alfredo sauce, basil, and garlic.
  3. Serve with preferred amount of salt, pepper and garlic. Enjoy!
